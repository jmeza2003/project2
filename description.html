What is a Habanero?

When you hear about the Habanero pepper you most likely think about a very hot pepper and well you’re not wrong.“The Red Savina Habanero” is currently the tenth most hottest pepper as of 2018. Its name says it . IT’S A RED HABANERO PEPPER.The habanero pepper comes in many colors.It is said that unripe habaneros are green and the most common color for mature habaneros are orange and red.The habanero peppers scoville unit is around 200,000 and 350,00 units.The habanero pepper was originally from the amazon regions of South Africa .But they died in frost. Now they are grown in states all over mexico such as : Tabasco,Campeche,Quintana Roo,Sonora,Veracruz,Chiapas and Yucatan is the state that produces the most habanero peppers.
